We did a food tasting there and we were surprised by how the good everything tastes. The pasta is just ah-mazing! We had the pesto one with haloumi cheese and the one with fine cheese. The honey and almond wings are also really good and the appetizer, pork pate with cranberries was just different. Also the server was really communicative and smiley which made our experience really nice. Will definitely come back and also recommend this place. Good job!
